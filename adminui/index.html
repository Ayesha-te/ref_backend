System Overview

How the Global Pool System Works

  • Deposit Approval: Each time an admin approves and credits a user deposit, exactly $0.50 USD is automatically added to the global pool.
  • Weekly Distribution: Every Monday, the entire global pool balance is distributed equally among all approved users.
  • Calculation Example: If 20 deposits were approved this week → Pool = 20 × $0.50 = $10.00. If there are 100 approved users → Each user gets $10 ÷ 100 = $0.10 USD ≈ 28 PKR.
  • Withdrawal Tax: A 20% tax is applied to global pool payouts before they are added to user wallets.
  • Automatic Process: The distribution happens automatically every Monday, and the net amount (after tax) is credited to each user's available balance.

Platform Economics

  • User Share: 80% of each deposit goes to the user's earning balance for passive income.
  • Global Pool: Fixed $0.50 per approval goes to the global pool for weekly distribution.
  • Platform Operations: The remaining portion supports platform operations and maintenance.
  • Passive Earnings: Users earn daily passive income on their balance according to the schedule (0.4% to 1.3% daily).
  • Referral System: Users earn from referrals: 6% (L1), 3% (L2), 1% (L3) of signup fees.

Tax Structure

  • Withdrawal Tax: 20% on all withdrawals (including global pool payouts).
  • No Tax on Deposits: Deposits are split according to the economics but no additional tax.
  • Referral Earnings: Paid directly to user wallets without additional tax.

How It Works

1) Daily Passive Income

  • Days 1–10: 0.4% daily
  • Days 11–20: 0.6% daily
  • Days 21–30: 0.8% daily
  • Days 31–60: 1.0% daily
  • Days 61–90: 1.3% daily

Passive income starts once you make your first credited deposit (investment). Your balance then grows daily per the schedule above.

Product Purchases

  • Logged-in users get a 10% discount on every product.
  • Guests can order without logging in (no discount).
  • At checkout, you’ll see the admin’s bank details and you’ll provide your Transaction ID, name, phone, and email.

2) Referral Earnings (per signup only)

  • Direct (Level 1): 6% of the signup payment at approval.
  • Indirect (Level 2): 3% of the signup payment at approval.
  • Indirect (Level 3): 1% of the signup payment at approval.

You don’t need 10 directs to start earning. You will earn on your first few referrals too (1st, 2nd, etc.).

3) Milestone Bonuses (Directs)

Earn a one‑time bonus when your directs reach each milestone count. We wait until those directs have completed their first investment. The bonus is a percentage of the combined first‑investment amounts of those directs. After each payout, the counter resets to 0 and the next milestone starts.

  • 10 directs → 1% of the combined first investments
  • 0–30 directs → when you reach 30, 3% of the combined first investments (then reset)
  • 0–100 directs → when you reach 100, 5% of the combined first investments (then reset)

4) Global Payouts

How it works:

  • On every Monday, $0.50 USD is deducted from the joining payment of users who joined on that Monday and added to the global pool.
  • Then, the entire pool is distributed equally among all approved users on the same Monday.
  • Example: 20 new users joined on Monday → Pool = 20 × $0.50 = $10.00
  • If 100 approved users at payout: Each user gets $10 ÷ 100 = $0.10 USD ≈ 28 PKR
  • A 20% withdrawal tax is applied, so the net amount is added to your wallet.

Your share is automatically added to your wallet every Monday after the tax deduction.

5) Withdrawals

  • Your daily earnings accumulate in your wallet.
  • Withdraw tax: 20% (net amount is credited to you).

6) Signup Deposit Note

The initial deposit made at signup is recorded for your transaction history, but it is not counted as earnings and does not affect your ROI.
